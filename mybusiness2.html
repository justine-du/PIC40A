δ About Us δ

Hello! My name is Justine Du, proud owner of a newly established decorating food company called Justine's® Estehetic Eatery. I am currently attending UCLA as a Cognitive Science major with a Specialization in Computing, and my favorite thing in the world is food and aesthetic-looking stuff. Combining the two, my goal is to bring that same visual and emotional happiness to you!





 How did we come about?


It seems like there has always been a separation between aesthetics and food: food was just meant to be eaten and pretty pictures and things were just meant to be looked at. However, I had a new idea of making beautifully decorated things edible as well to enhance the enjoyment of people all over the world. To be able to customize your own colors and textures and make food your own creation was revolutionary.


I want to bring that same enjoyment to you and your family, to create and customize your work of art into something that can not only be admired but also bring joy to your hungry stomachs as well! Our company is set on creating your perfect cake, hamburger, pizza, or whatever you can think of to your doorstep, so come and order today!
